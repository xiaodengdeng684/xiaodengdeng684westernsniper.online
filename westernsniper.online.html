About Western Sniper

Western Sniper places you in the dusty boots of a skilled marksman in the Wild West. With your trusty rifle in hand, you'll face numerous challenges that will test your accuracy, timing, and nerve.

Set against the backdrop of frontier towns, deserts, and canyons, you'll experience the thrill of being an elite sharpshooter in a lawless land where only the quickest and most precise survive.

The game combines stunning visuals with intuitive controls, making it accessible for newcomers while offering depth for experienced players seeking to master every challenge.

Game Features

  • Multiple challenging levels across diverse Wild West environments
  • Realistic shooting mechanics and physics
  • Various mission types to test different skills
  • Upgradable weapons and equipment
  • Compete for high scores with players worldwide

How to Play

Use your mouse or touchscreen to aim and shoot at targets. Time your shots carefully and watch out for obstacles and moving targets.

Complete objectives in each level to progress through the story and unlock new challenges and equipment.

Adjust your strategy based on environmental conditions like wind and distance for the perfect shot!
